Featured Episode

With Network Bits

In our first episode of the Blockchain Bay, Chris Bagnell and Network Bits discuss Crypto Projects they were interested in 2021 such as Helium, Deeper, PlanetWatch and more that did not pan out to what they anticipated. They discussed Projects they are looking forward to like PiPhi, and Functional NFTs, As well as the pros and cons of Staking Crypto. They also talked about the Crypto Community as a whole with their pros and cons.
